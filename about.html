Enable.ps

ENABLE.ps is a pioneering community-based consultancy firm that provides professional services in entrepreneurship, business development, institutional building, strategic planning, capacity building, marketing, digital solutions, and logistics—all aligned with international quality standards.

Following a comprehensive assessment of the service market, ENABLE.ps expanded its vision and scope to meet the growing demand for technical and professional consultancy services. The company was officially registered in 2022 under the name ENABLE.ps, offering a wide range of services to organizations, institutions, and individuals seeking to achieve their goals. ENABLE.ps is committed to preserving its core values and presenting itself as a trusted advisor.

The company brings together a distinguished group of experts, consultants, and trainers specializing in management, economics, and media, who continuously strive to enhance clients’ performance by identifying their needs and helping them grow.

What Makes ENABLE.psUnique:

  • Deep understanding of the labor market and service landscape.
  • A participatory planning approach that involves engaging with clients and stakeholders to assess inputs and outcomes, and design interventions that align with client goals and aspirations.
  • A wide range of professional expertise in consultancy, business development, capacity building, and logistics to ensure high efficiency and impact.
  • A qualified and diverse team of consultants with extensive experience in institutional development, business planning, quality assurance, and strategic management—delivering integrated, high-quality services based on global best practices.
