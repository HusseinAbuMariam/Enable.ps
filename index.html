ENABLE.ps for Consultancy and Logistic Services

ENABLE.ps Consulting and Logistics Services is a leading community-based consulting firm that provides consulting in the fields of entrepreneurship, business development, and institutional building and development, including planning, management, capacity building, marketing, digital solutions, and logistics services, in accordance with international quality standards:

  • Consultancy and logistical services
  • Institutional development
  • Strategic planning
  • Feasibility studies
  • Quality management system development
  • Project management and evaluation
  • Monitoring and follow-up

The company's idea and operations began in 2017 with the launch of a pioneering project called "Play and Learn with Ghadeer," which created educational tools based on educational and creative ideas for teachers and parents, embodying the principle of learning through play and fun.
